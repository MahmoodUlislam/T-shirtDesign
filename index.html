Design a T-shit that will make us proud

The T-shirt design competition is open to all students enrolled in the Web Design and Development as well as the Interactive Design and Technology program at Saskatchewan Polytechnic. The winning design will be printed on a T-shirt and worn by the Web Development students at the 2021 SaskPolytech Graduation Ceremony.

Parameters to follow for the design

  • The design must be original and unique.
  • The design must be submitted in a vector format (Adobe Illustrator, Corel Draw, etc.).
  • The design must be submitted in a high resolution format (300 dpi).
  • The design must be submitted in artwork format (CMYK) also.
  • The design must be fitted on the T-shirt.

You need to be a student of Saskatchewan Polytechnic to attend this competition.
